What "production testing" means here — two levels

Be clear which test you're preparing for; they have very different readiness bars.

DiscoveryDeploy + backfill, then browse a real, evidenced corpus of horror/sci-fi financiers in the dashboard. Reachable after Sections 1–3.
The productA qualified, deduplicated, ranked, contactable list — the actual deliverable. Needs Sections 4–5 (the Claude classifier + contacts).
OutreachActually emailing/calling anyone. Hard-gated on Section 6 compliance — do not test outreach before it passes.
